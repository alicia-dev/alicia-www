Meet Amberile

Amberile is an open-source, lightweight and multi-platform game framework.

FEATURES

• Cross-platform


This doesn't mean written-once run-everywhere. However, you can port your game to any supported platform within few tweaks.

• Software Render


It might be a bit slow, but you will get same result regardless of device the game is running on.

• Independence


Amberile doesn't rely much on 3rd-party libraries. But you still need the standard and OS-specified ones.

• Open-source


It's free as in freedom. You may sell the game you made. You can even sell Amberile itself.

• Easy & Simple


It's extreme easy to get started. You don't have to learn through a very huge document of functions.

• Data-driven


The data itself control the flow. Not the logic nor the objects.
